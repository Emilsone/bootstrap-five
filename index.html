About Me

Hey! Welcome here. About me? You probably know all about me from listening to my music. Don’t listen to my music? Well, this is a step in the right direction. My name is Walker Johnny, a RnB singer with the stagename “JohnnyB”. I started singing since i was 5 years old, but i started singing officially in 2018. After my debut album Infinite (2019), I signed with Turner Beacons record and subsequently achieved mainstream popularity in late 2019 with Butterflies EP. I have since then released other music which includes Dont leave me, Love you Baby amongst others.
